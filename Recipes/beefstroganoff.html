Beef Stroganoff with Ground Beef

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1/3 cup chopped onion
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon seasoning salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/3 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 3/4 cup sour cream, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon liquid smoke, or to taste (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add mushrooms and onions to saute until onions have turned translucent, about 5 minutes
  3. Add ground beef and cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes
  4. Sprinkle with flour, garlic powder, seasoning salt, paprika and pepper;stir until mixed in
  5. Add condensed soup, sour cream, liquid smoke, and worcestershire sauce
  6. Reduce heat to medium, cook and stire until sauce has thickened, 5 to 7 minutes
