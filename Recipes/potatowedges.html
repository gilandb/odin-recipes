Wholesome Potato Wedges made in Air Fryer

Ingredients

  • 2 medium russet potatoes
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon parsley flages
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 C)
  2. Cut each potato in half lengthwise. Cut each half in half lengthwise, then cut each quarter in half lengthwise
    You will have 16 wedges
  3. Place potato wedges in a large bowl. Add olive oil, paprika, parsley, chili, salt, and pepper
    mix until well combined
  4. Place 1/2 of the potato wedges in teh basket of the air fryer and cook for 10 minutes
  5. Flip wedges with tongs and cook an additional 5 minutes.
    Remove to plate
  6. Repeat to cook remaining wedges
